2024 Election Model

A highly accurate attempt at modelling the 2024 Election

This was my fairly simple attempt at creating my own Presidential election model. I scraped RCP using Selenium with the simulation being a state-level Monte Carlo election forecast. In the end, I ended up with a model that produced estimates in line with the mainstream betting markets and models as well as accurately predicted the vote share in 6 of 7 swing states.
